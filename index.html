RESEARCH PARTICIPATION

Study Title: Understanding Economic Fundamentals Single-Question Survey

We are independent researchers conducting an email-based study, which we would like to invite you to take part in. This website exists to share with you important information about the study. Participation is entirely voluntary.

Purpose of the Study

The purpose of the study is to learn more about the beliefs of experts and non-experts in the field of Economics. It is part of a larger project that aims to develop new research methods, suitable to the contemporary organization of social life.

What will I do if I choose to be in this study?

You will be asked to answer a single multiple choice question.

After completing the single-question survey, you will have the opportunity to comment on the survey if you wish to do so. We welcome feedback of any type.

Your participation should take no more than one minute. The single question survey is administered online. It may be completed at your convenience, at a location of your choice.

Possible risks and benefits

We believe that the risks involved in this study are minimal and that the activities involved present no more risk of harm than you would experience in everyday life.

Protection of personal information

Results of this study may be used in publications and presentations. The information you provide will be handled as confidentially as possible. We will not collect or retain any personally-identifying information. If results of this study are published or presented, your name and other personally identifiable information will not be used, nor will the list of participants be published. Data on the institutional affiliations of participants may be published only in aggregate form.

Financial Information

Participation in this study will involve no cost to you. You will not be paid for participating in this study.

What are my rights as a research participant?

Your participation in this study is voluntary. If at any time and for any reason, you would prefer not to participate in this study, please feel free not to. You will not be penalized in any way.

Please note that this study has not been approved by an Institutional Review Board.
